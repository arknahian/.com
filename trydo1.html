THE FUTURE IS CALLING

Being a graduate student at Westcollege means being a part of an energetic, dynamic group of people who are passionate, curious and committed to their areas of study. 
We take great pride in ensuring that our graduate students, who represent 106 countries, have a full yet balanced experience during their time here.

our courses

World Literature

Our course in World Literature will give you the opportunity to explore a range of international literature from the 18th century to present times.

Contemporary Literature

The goals of this course is to become familiar with the very best works of fiction, non-fiction, drama, and poetry of the last twenty years.

Writing and Rhetoric

During the course, students will learn to analyze persuasive writing strategies and make effective rhetorical choices in their own work.
